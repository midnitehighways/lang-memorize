be a polyglote
WWWelcome! This app might be helpful to language learners as well as polyglottic enthusiasts, since it's first aim is to help storing and memorizing new words and expressions. Log in through one of your social network accounts and create your own vocabulary. Or don't log in and just join the common word database. You will be able to add/edit/delete words and fill in corresponding examples. Besides, once in a while you may test you knowledge. Basic settings for your vocabulary are also available. More details below.
Just add w... wwwords
Excellent tool to store the newly learned words and expressions. Just come to 'Add new' section , insert it into database and never lost it! Don't forget to specify the type of your word (e.g. noun, verb, etc.), although it's not necessary.
One wwword is never enough
Don't limit yourself to simple original-translation combination. Apart from word type you can provide an infinite number of example expressions for each of your words. Click on the needed line in the word table and it'll expand. There you can also update or delete your words.
First logic, then wwwords
Organize your vocabulary in a clever way. Logged in you've got a possibility to either include your own words/expressions/examples in the common database or hide it from the public. Specify it in settings . In the same section you can choose the scope of your test (common vocabulary vs. own words).
Log in to WWWords
Tired of all this boring registrations, account confirmations and password remembering? Your browser can't hold already all the web passwords? Well, no such stuff here! Just log in using your social media credentials: . And be sure, it's safe. None of your personal data at any circumstances will be exposed to author or anybody else!
WWWords testing
Stored a bunch of expressions and now you are struggling memorizing it? WWWords app is ready to help. Take a small test and check your knowledge. And don't forget about test settings .
P.S. More test types to come later.
Setting the wwwords up
Tired of same dull background? Or don't want to show words from own vocabulary in public storage? Wanna add to your testing scope common words? Or maybe want to style/animate somehow the test area? Drop in on already mentioned 'Settings' section!
A bit more about author
Hi, I'm Alexandru, the author of WWWords. The idea of such tool appeared when I first came to Finland in 2013 and threw myself into learning Finnish. I tried to memorize as much words as I could, so I wrote'em down every day in my exercise book, PC, phone, textbook, on some random papers... That was just wrong. Trying to centralize somehow the storing location, I wrote the first, primitive version of this app using JS/PHP/MySQL.

Time passed by and by the end of 2015 it was decided to completely rewrite this program from scratch using Django/PostgreSQL, since at that time I was in love with Python. (Well, I am now too.) Thus, for me this app became a natural coldblooded aiming at two birds with one stone: I wanted to improve both Django and Finnish skills:)

WWWords are not 100% ready yet, I keep working upon this website as a part of my self-studying process. But all the options and tools available here are fully functional. Note, that personal vocabularies and the whole database will stay untouched, since I make backups periodically. So, please feel free to use it! If you have any suggestions or ideas, mail me or send me a message. Please, find all my contacts in footer sections.

P.S. And don't be afraid of some random words in the common vocabulary. These are the part of testing process. As for April'16 at least three persons - voluntary testers - stored their recently learned expressions there.
